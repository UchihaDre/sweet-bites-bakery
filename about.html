About Sweet Bites Bakery

Sweet Bites Bakery was founded in Nairobi with a simple goal: to provide freshly baked goods made from quality ingredients which were sourced locallyand at affordable prices.

Our bakery specializes in artisan breads, celebration cakes, pastries, cookies,tea scones, buns, muffins and custom orders for birthdays, weddings, and corporate events.

We are committed to excellent customer service and creating products that bring people together through great food.

Our Story

Sweet Bites Bakery was established in 2018 by a family of passionate bakers who wanted to bring freshly baked, high-quality products to the Nairobi community.

Starting as a small neighbourhood bakery, we quickly gained a reputation for our artisan breads, custom cakes, and delicious pastries made from carefully selected ingredients.

Today, Sweet Bites Bakery serves hundreds of customers every week, providing fresh products for families, schools, offices, weddings, birthdays, and other special occasions.

Our mission remains simple: to create baked goods that bring people together while maintaining the highest standards of quality, freshness, and customer service.
